# Superstore Sales Dashboard

Analysis of retail sales data to identify top-performing regions,
products, and monthly trends using Python.

## What I did
- Loaded and cleaned 8,399 sales transactions across 21 columns
- Analyzed total sales by region, product category, and month
- Identified the top 10 best-selling products by revenue
- Visualized a monthly sales trend from 2009 to 2012

## Key findings
- The West region generated the highest total sales (£3.6M)
- Office Supplies is the largest category by order volume (4,610 orders)
- Nunavut had the lowest regional sales — a potential growth opportunity

## Tools used
Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Jupyter Notebook
